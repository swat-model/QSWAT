◆ isBatch

QSWAT.hrus.BasinData.isBatch

Map of crop to area of crop in subbasin.

This and the similar maps for soil and slope are duplicated: an original version created after basin data is calculated and before HRUs are created, and another after HRUs are created. Original crop area map Map of soil to area of soil in subbasin. Original soil area map Map of slope to area of slope in subbasin. Original slope area map Flag to show if batch run
